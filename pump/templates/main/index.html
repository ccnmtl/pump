Many people with limb loss have difficulties with balance and mobility that makes home and community activities challenging and increases the risk of falls. Understanding the risk factors that affect your ability to safely participate in life’s activities is an important step in reducing your risk of falls and injury while optimizing your walking potential.

Complete the Risk Inventory survey and receive personalized recommendations about

  • Your current walking ability category
  • Your Prosthetic Use for Mobility Prognosis (PUMP) for future community walking
  • Tips for Injury Prevention Strategies (TIPS) to help you avoid falls and injury
